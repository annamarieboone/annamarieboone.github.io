about me (anna marie boone)

I've been a part of the journalism world since 2009, when I took my first writing & reporting class. Currently, I am working at the Indiana Daily Student as graphics editor and design chief. I'm always looking for large data projects to report, such as when I counted and categorized every bill in the Indiana General Assembly for this legislative year.

Outside the newsroom I can be found hiking, camping, taking impromptu road trips, playing with dogs or sitting on a porch (not necessarily in that order).
