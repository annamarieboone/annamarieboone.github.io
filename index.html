I'm a senior (in denial) at Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana. I'm currently working as a design intern at the Denver Post in Colorado. In the fall I'll serve as creative director for the Indiana Daily Student, covering a historical presidential election where Indiana is actually pretty relevant. Previously, I've served as weekend editor for the IDS, as well as a design chief, graphics editor and digital editor.

When I'm not at my official work, I can often be found laying in a hammock like it's my real job, hiking, kayaking, reading or sleeping. My ideal day involves a combination of all five of those activities + beer. I'm currently reading "The Circle" by Dave Eggers and listening to anything by Modern Baseball.
