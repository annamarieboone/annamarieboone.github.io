website development and design, illustrations & animation

Created the storify and wrote the headline and introduction about IDS staff members' experiences with sexual assault. These tweets from our editors and reporters were part of a campaign by our staff to try and get more students to come forward and share their stories about sexual assault. I organized the tweets, coming up with the format of quoting our tweets from staff members' personal accounts with their name and position from the IDS account. This format helped get the IDS brand on every tweet while still giving readers the link to the personal account of our staff members, which was important in showing we are humans, not just a faceless media organization.

worked with James Benedict to develop and design a website to live on the IDS website during winter break, highlighting the most newsworthy stories from the past semester.

About me | What I do and why I do it

I'm a senior (in denial) at Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana. I'm currently serving as the creative director for the Indiana Daily Student, managing the staff and covering a historical presidential election where Indiana is actually pretty relevant. The past summer I was a design intern at the Denver Post in Colorado. Previously, I've served as weekend editor, design chief, graphics editor and digital editor for the IDS. I've also worked as an editorial and web intern in London, England for Verge Magazine and Common Room PR.
