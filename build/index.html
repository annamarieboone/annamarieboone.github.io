hi 👋 i’m anna marie boone,

I'm a designer at the Minnesota Star Tribune, where I use code and persistance to create visual stories.

I'm an adjunct professor at the University of St. Thomas, where I teach an advanced media design course for the Department of Emerging Media.

I'm on the computer a lot for both of those roles, so when I'm not working, I try to stay off of it. I do so by going for runs with my dog (who vastly outpaces me), speed-reading book club books at the end of the month and knitting sweaters.

My work has been honored by the Society for News Design, where I was a finalist for World's Best Designer, the Pulitzer Prizes and the National Headliner Awards. I was also named Young Journalist of the Year by the Minnesota chapter of the Society for Professional Journalists.

That's me ✌️
